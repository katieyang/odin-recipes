Fried Rice

Home

Description

Delicious tomato fried rice, very easy to make. Was shocked to find out adding ketchup to a tomato dish actually makes it taste much better.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Hoisin
  • Ketchup
  • Flour
  • Meat of choice (e.g., sausage, ham, etc.)

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and leave it to cool.
  2. Heat the cooking oil and scramble some eggs, then set eggs aside.
  3. Heat the cooking oil and fry the garlic.
  4. Throw in tomatoes to fry.
  5. Add hoisin, ketchup, and some flour.
  6. Once tomatoes look cooked, throw in the rice and eggs and meat.
